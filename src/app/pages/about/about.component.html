About Denhac

denhac is a 501(c)3 organization, supported by a community of volunteers and members who contribute to the space with memberships, donations, and time.

Denhac is a vibrant and growing community. Come visit us at one of our open houses on Tuesday nights at 7:00 PM. If you like what you see, please feel free to donate money or equipment, or become a member for unlimited 24x7 access to the space and all the awesomeness inside!
